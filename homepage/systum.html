Create a question paper

Choose from four distinct models to generate a question paper. Each model has a brief description explaining its format or specialty.

Model 1

General Question Paper

Choose this model if you want to generate a general question paper based on the course material. The questions will be randomly selected from the course content.

Model 2

Custom Question Paper

Choose this model if you want to create a custom question paper. You can specify the number of questions, the difficulty level, and the topic. The questions will be based on your input.

Model 3

Practice Question Paper

Choose this model if you want to generate a practice question paper. The questions will be based on the course material, but with a focus on key concepts and skills. You can specify the number of questions and the difficulty level.

Model 4

Exam Question Paper

Choose this model if you want to generate an exam question paper. The questions will be based on the course material, with a focus on testing your understanding and application of key concepts and skills. You can specify the number of questions and the difficulty level.
